About

The purpose of this website is to inform you about the history of video games and the development of video game consoles. You will be given information on the video game consoles Microsoft, Nintendo, and Sony have developed and the most popular franchises from each company's video game consoles. Also, trivia relating to each company is given to test your knowledge. Are you a hardcore Nintendo, Microsoft, or Sony specialist?

Is this Website for You?

If you are someone who's interested in video games or want to learn more about the general history of video games and the development of video game consoles, this is the website for you! This website will give you informative, interesting, and surprising information in relation to video games and video game consoles.
